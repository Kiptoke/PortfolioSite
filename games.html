Games

Located here you will see all the games that I have made over the years, both from game jams and my own personal projects.

Ok, ZoomersJune 2020

A casual endless runner game, where you pilot a spaceship zooming through a crowded asteroid field.

While the game jam was a week long, I was busy with managing and running the jam; As a result, Ok, Zoomers was made in only one day using the Godot Engine.

Made for WolverineSoft's June Jam. The theme was "Zoom."

Danger DuckJune 2020

A 2D top-down shooter built with the Godot Engine.

The game jam encouraged developers to make games with "bad" art. I decided to crowdsource graphics from my close friends, and put them into the game. All of their graphics and attributations are available in the credits.

Made for the "I Can't Draw But Want to Make a Game (Again)" game jam on Itch.io.

Itch.io Link

IoApril 2020 - UI Designer

A 2D action platformer where a girl with teleportation powers descends into the abyss to uncover a mystery surrounding her village. Made as a part of a three-month project in Unity.

I was a UI Designer working with WolverineSoft Studio, the University of Michigan's long-term game dev team.

Itch.io Link

Re-PairFebruary 2020

"On a beautiful day, two blocks, RED and BLUE head out hiking on a canyon trail - they pass over a gorge on a small rope bridge, when it suddenly gives way with BLUE on it! BLUE finds themself deep down within in the canyon, and it sounds like a rogue river flood is coming up..."

This game was my first use of the Godot Engine, along with my first use of Pyxel Edit and MilkyTracker in a game-dev setting.

Made for the 2020 Global Game Jam at the University of Michigan. The theme was "Repair."
